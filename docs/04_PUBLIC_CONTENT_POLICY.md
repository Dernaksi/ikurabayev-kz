# Public Content Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines what may be published through IKurabayev.kz and what must be
excluded from the repository and website.

## Publication Standard

All public content should be accurate, reviewable, and suitable for publication
on a personal research and engineering website. Do not publish claims that cannot
be verified from public or explicitly approved sources.

## Allowed Public Content

- Public biography and professional summary.
- Public research interests.
- Public project and engineering portfolio descriptions.
- Public publications, presentations, or links when approved for sharing.
- Public social, academic, or professional links.
- QR landing page content intended for broad sharing.
- Multilingual public content strategy for English, Russian, and Kazakh.

## Restricted Content

Do not add:

- Private phone numbers or private addresses.
- Tokens, passwords, API keys, SSH keys, secrets, or `.env` files.
- Contracts, financial records, or private institutional documents.
- Unpublished research data, raw datasets, or confidential manuscripts.
- Private correspondence or unapproved personal information.
- Fabricated metrics, roles, affiliations, publications, figures, or results.

## Research Claims

Research claims must be grounded in public or approved sources. If a claim needs
supporting evidence, add a note that evidence is required instead of presenting
the claim as final.

## Contact Strategy

Prefer public, intentionally shared contact routes. Do not add private phone
numbers or private addresses. Contact details should be reviewed before
publication.

## Multilingual Strategy

The future site may support English, Russian, and Kazakh. Translations should be
reviewed for accuracy, tone, and consistency. Do not invent translated claims
that are stronger than the approved source content.
